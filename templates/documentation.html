Credit Intelligence Framework

A definitive guide to the standardization, harmonization, and monitoring of institutional credit instruments.

1. The Normalization Pipeline

01

Ingestion

Bespoke Credit Agreements in PDF format are uploaded to the secure danio vault for initial OCR processing.

02

Harmonization

Varied legal phrasing and commercial terms are resolved into a unified framework (e.g., standardizing "Spread over SOFR").

03

SLO Generation

The system generates a Standardized Loan Object—a machine-readable record of all facility attributes.

04

Ledger Integration

Final SLOs are committed to the immutable Credit Ledger for real-time portfolio tracking and auditability.

2. Glossary of Terms

SLO (Standardized Loan Object)
The fundamental data unit representing a single credit facility mapped to a universal schema.
Agreement Normalization
Aligning diverse legal phrasing into a consistent commercial framework across jurisdictions.
Compliance Breach Alert
Real-time triggers activated when an SLO’s calculated metrics exceed extracted financial covenants.

3. Standard Attributes

  • Object ID Unique alphanumeric tracker for the instrument lifecycle.
  • Pricing Margin Standardized spread over base rate (e.g., S+350bps).
  • Leverage Covenant Primary compliance threshold (Max Debt/EBITDA).
